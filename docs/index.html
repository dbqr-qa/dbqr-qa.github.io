DBQR-QA: A Shared Task
DBQR-QA
A Question Answering Dataset on a Hybrid of Database Querying and Reasoning
Available 2 September 2024
A SHARED TASK
TLDR
Generate programs to answer a series of questions.
WHAT YOU GET
400 questions, 10 for each conversation, with tables (Pandas Data Frame) queried from our graph database.
YOUR MODEL GENERATES
Programs that answer each question using our pre-defined functions or your own programs in any language.
WHAT EVALUATE
The answer your model generated (number, text, set, or table).
QUESTION 1
What is the ratio of combined revenues reported by companies in the retail industry with liabilities between 100M and 500M every year during 2017 and 2020 to those with liabilities between 300M and 1B during the same years?
QUESTION 4
If I removed the top three companies by combined deferred tax liabilities from the first group, what would the average be?
